About Eze

Eze is a picturesque, medieval mountaintop village in the French Riviera.

Location

Eze is located on the French Riviera and is approximately midway between the City of Nice and Monaco.

Map it!

Attractions

Village

The village was colonized by the Romans. It is believed they built a temple to the Egyptian goddess Isis, which may be the origin of the town's name.

Gardens

At the top of the village is the Jardin Exotique which is 1310 feet above sea level and features a collection of exotique trees and plants. From the garden, on a clear day, it is possible to see all the way to Italy and St. Tropez.
